Care Guide

Freshness and storage

A few simple habits help microgreens stay crisp, bright, and enjoyable for as long as possible.

Best practices

  • Refrigerate promptly after pickup.
  • Keep greens dry and cold until ready to use.
  • Use a clean, dry utensil or hand when handling them.
  • Enjoy them within a few days of harvest for the best flavor and texture.

Simple use ideas

  • Sandwiches and wraps
  • Salads and grain bowls
  • Eggs, tacos, and soups
  • Smoothies and light garnishes
  • Chef plating and finishing touches
