Accessibility

Accessibility commitment

Sustainable Roots Collective wants this website to be usable, understandable, and welcoming for as many people as possible.

What this means in practice

  • Clear page structure and readable headings
  • Meaningful link text and navigation labels
  • Keyboard-focus visibility for interactive elements
  • Form labels and straightforward contact routes
  • Ongoing attention to readability and contrast
