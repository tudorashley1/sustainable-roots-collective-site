Frequently asked questions

A simple guide to how Sustainable Roots Collective works, what microgreens are, and what to expect when you reach out.

What are microgreens?

Microgreens are young edible greens harvested soon after sprouting. They are known for fresh flavor, color, and versatility in everyday meals and professional kitchens.

How do I order?

Right now, ordering is inquiry-based. Use the contact page to ask about current availability. Brian will reply with what is available, expected timing, and next-step details for pickup or supply coordination.

Do you have a storefront?

Not at this time. Sustainable Roots Collective currently operates through direct inquiries, local pickup, restaurant conversations, and seasonal market appearances.

Where is pickup?

Pickup details are shared directly when availability is confirmed. This keeps the process simple and aligned with current harvests.

Do you deliver?

Delivery can be discussed depending on the type of order and location, but the current model is designed around simple local pickup unless otherwise arranged.

Do you supply restaurants?

Yes. Local restaurant inquiries are welcome. Sustainable Roots Collective is especially interested in supply relationships that value consistency, freshness, and clear communication.

How long do microgreens stay fresh?

They are best enjoyed within a few days of harvest for the best flavor and texture. Proper cold storage helps extend freshness. See the Freshness page for care tips.

What varieties are available?

Availability may vary. Common examples can include sunflower, pea shoots, broccoli, radish, and chef-friendly blends depending on the season and current grow cycle.

Still have a question?

If your question is not covered here, use the contact page and Brian will get back to you with availability, ordering details, or next steps.
