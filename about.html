About Sustainable Roots Collective

Built by a grower who cares about doing things well.

Sustainable Roots Collective is Brian’s way of bringing together his love of plants, hands-on work, and fresh local food into something real and useful.

The Story

Brian has spent the last 15 years working in manufacturing, where consistency, process, and follow-through matter. At the same time, he has always loved agriculture, growing, and the kind of hands-on work that turns care into something you can actually see.

Sustainable Roots Collective grew out of that intersection. It is a local microgreens business built around freshness, practical discipline, and the simple belief that good food should feel alive, close to home, and worth sharing.

He is also a father of two daughters, and part of what drives this work is the desire to build something rooted, honest, and full of possibility for the future.

The goal is simple: grow fresh, serve well, keep the quality high, and let the business grow at a pace that can actually be sustained.

What Matters Here

The brand has personality, but the work stays grounded in freshness, consistency, and trust.

Freshness

Microgreens are grown and shared with freshness at the center, not treated like commodity produce.

Care

Small-batch growing leaves room for attention, consistency, and pride in the finished product.

Community

The focus is local: homes, chefs, and markets in and around Muskegon.

Who It Is For

  • Households that want fresh, easy additions for everyday meals
  • Local chefs looking for vibrant greens with flavor, color, and texture
  • Market shoppers who value local growing and direct connection

What Comes Next

The business is being built carefully, with a focus on reliable growing, clear communication, and a simple customer experience. That means starting with what can be fulfilled well, then expanding from there.
