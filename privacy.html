Privacy policy

This page explains what information may be collected through this website and how it is used.

Information collected

If you use the contact form, Sustainable Roots Collective may collect the information you choose to submit, such as your name, email address, phone number, and the details of your message.

How it is used

Submitted information is used to respond to your inquiry, discuss availability, coordinate pickup or restaurant supply questions, and provide the information you requested.

What is not intended

Your information is not collected for unrelated marketing purposes without your request. It is not intended to be sold to third parties.

Communication

If you ask about availability, ordering, or supply, Sustainable Roots Collective may follow up using the contact information you provide.

Questions about privacy
