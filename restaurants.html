For local kitchens

Fresh microgreens for chefs who care about color, texture, and consistency.

Sustainable Roots Collective offers small-batch local microgreens for restaurants in and around Muskegon. The focus is freshness, reliability, and a simple working relationship.

Why Work Together

  • Fresh, local microgreens harvested close to use
  • Strong color and texture for garnish, plating, sandwiches, bowls, and salads
  • Clear communication about availability and lead time
  • Small-batch supply built around care, not volume theater

Likely Varieties

Availability may shift as the grow rhythm develops, but inquiries are welcome for common favorites such as sunflower, pea shoots, broccoli, radish, and chef-forward blends.

Custom requests may be possible depending on timing, consistency, and volume.

What to Expect
